About Me

I'm Parag Bagul, and I'm passionate about web and mobile application security. With almost 2 years of experience in the industry, I have reported security vulnerabilities to a variety of companies, including Apple, Oracle, Philips, McAfee, Byjus, Nykaa and many more.

As a security professional, my goal is to help organizations protect their assets and information from cyber threats. I specialize in web application pentesting, mobile application pentesting, and source code review.
